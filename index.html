Jonathan Karin

I am a researcher specializing in computational biology and graph signal processing. My work focuses on analyzing complex biological systems using advanced computational methods and mathematical modeling. I am particularly interested in understanding how biological networks function and evolve, and how we can leverage graph-based approaches to uncover hidden patterns in biological data.
